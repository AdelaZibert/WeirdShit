Danger Music

Danger music is a peculiar music genre, that originates form belief, that some music can or will hurt the listenr or the performer. It can include things like the members of the gand Hantarash driving a bulldozer through the venue of their performance, to odd instructions like directing the performer to gouge out one of his or her eyes five years from now.
