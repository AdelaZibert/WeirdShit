Mothman

Mothman is a moth-like creature that was being seen in Point Pleasant, Virginia between november 1966 and december 1967. The events have also been described in a book Mothman Prophecies and a movie made after the book.

Man from Taured

In 1954, a man arrived at a Tokyo airport, claiming to come form a country called Taured, even presenting a passport issued by the saind country. Not knowing what to do, they put him in a nearby hotel over night, from which he vanished the next morning along with his documents, leaving this mystery unsolved.
